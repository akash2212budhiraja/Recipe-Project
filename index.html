Shahi Paneer

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Shahi Paneer Masala
  • And Some Love

Process

  • Prepare Cashew Paste: Soak cashew nuts in warm water for about 30 minutes. Drain the water and blend the cashews into a smooth paste. Set aside.
  • Aromatics: Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add Ginger, Garlic, and Chilies: Add grated ginger, minced garlic, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add Spice Powders: Stir in coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Mix well and cook for a minute.
